# VibeHealth


## Base requirements draft
Help us draft a comprehensive roadmap to create a health app. 
My first though as to the features is the following:
- step, heart rate, sleep, speed, activity other vitals tracking with trends, avreges and warnings
- medicines tracking and reminders + details on their side effect (and you can add your own notes)
- health checks, vaccines, and personnalized reminders (depends on age, sex, medical condition, current medicine levels, estimated delay before appointment)
- workouts tab with exercices suggestions based on the profile
- a profiling step at the beginning
- mood tracker
- period tracker (with pills reminders)
- a map of the closest practioners with a search feature (P2)
- relaxation sounds/meditation guides
- focus helper (blocks everything and if leaves the bunny doesn't get its carrot)
- a bunny mascotte
- journalling (with locations, images, audio, videos etc, markdown style stuff)
- helpine if problem
- 1st aid guide and survival tips
- pollen tracking
- data sharing with relatives
- a medical ID with age/medication/emergency contacts etc
- pregnancy tracking with guides
- guides/articles for every conditions (based on the Vidal)
- export medical ID + other datas to other applications and to pdf and zip and csv
- sync with google and samsung and apple health (P2)
- calendar sync and doctolib sync for appointments
- nutrition and calories tracking
- hydration tracking
- workout and health goals
