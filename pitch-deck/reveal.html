Design Philosophy

🎨 Warm Coral Gradients & Organic Shapes
Creates a friendly, approachable aesthetic that reduces health anxiety and builds trust. Organic shapes feel more human and less clinical.
✨ Animated Micro-interactions
Bunny mascot and carrot XP animations provide positive reinforcement, encourage daily engagement, and make health tracking feel rewarding rather than tedious.
📝 Satoshi Typography & Pill Controls
Modern, readable typeface with generous spacing ensures clarity for all users including those with visual impairments. Pill-style buttons are intuitive and comfortable to tap.
♿ WCAG AA Accessibility & Dark Mode
Ensures the app is usable by everyone, including those with color blindness or visual disabilities. Dark mode reduces eye strain and accommodates user preferences.

Tech Stack

🎨 Frontend: Angular 21 + Tailwind CSS
Modern standalone component architecture, reactive signals, and utility-first styling for rapid development and maintainability.
⚡ Backend: Bun + Hono + Prisma
Bun provides ultra-fast JavaScript runtime, Hono offers minimal routing overhead, Prisma gives type-safe database access with excellent DX.
🗄️ Database: PostgreSQL
Production-grade RDBMS with strong ACID compliance, perfect for health data integrity and complex relational queries.
🔐 Auth: BetterAuth
Simplifies session and token management with built-in OAuth support, reducing security vulnerabilities in authentication.
🐳 Deploy: Docker & CI/CD
Containerization ensures consistency across environments. Automated pipelines reduce manual errors and accelerate deployment cycles.
