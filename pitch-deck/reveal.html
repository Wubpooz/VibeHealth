Unify vitals tracking, medical intelligence, wellness tools, and lifestyle features into one delightful PWA experience.

Offline-first • Bilingual (EN/FR) • Soft Pop design

The Problem

  • Fragmented health apps create data silos.
  • Medical info & first-aid context is hard to access quickly.
  • Personalization + persistence are inconsistent across existing tools.
  • Mobile health tracking is often inaccessible offline.

Our Solution

🎯 VibeHealth is an offline-first, bilingual personal health companion that centralizes vitals tracking, medical intelligence, wellness tools, and lifestyle features—all delivered with a premium "Soft Pop" aesthetic, a bunny mascot, and a carrot-based reward system.

  • Clinically strong tracking + wellness coaching.
  • Data ownership and privacy first design.

Core Features

1. Medical ID + QR export

2. First-aid guide, steps, and protection in offline mode

3. Vitals logging: blood pressure, heart rate, weight, glucose

4. Activity and nutrition tracking with reference catalog

5. Goals and progress + carrot rewards

6. Journal, sleep, mood, wellness reminders

7. Personalized insights and trend graphs

8. Secure auth + account sync

9. Multi-device PWA install, dark mode, animations
